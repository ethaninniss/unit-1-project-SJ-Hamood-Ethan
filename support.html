How can you contribute?

Our mission here at Safe Haven Everywhere is to provide housing to homeless LGBTQ people. We also provide housing for other homeless people awell ouside of the LBGTQ community. Safe Haven Everywhere is a non-profit organization. Our organization on runs donations from contributors who love what we do. We not only accept donations of a monetary value but we also accept donations in form of food and clothes.
