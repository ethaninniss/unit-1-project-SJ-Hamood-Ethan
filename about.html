Our Mission

Our mission here at Safe Haven Everywhere is to provide comfortable housing, food, and clothing to homeless people. Whilst we accept all types of homesless people we mainly focus on the LGBTQ community of homeless people as they are more often neglected then others.

Our Funding

Safe Haven Everywhere is a non profit organization. We run off of donations from genrous contributers. All donations made are focused on solely on our project to help the homeless.
